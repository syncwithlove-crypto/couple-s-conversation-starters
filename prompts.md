# Couples Conversation Prompts

A human-readable list of all prompts organized by category.

---

## Romantic

1. What small thing do I do that makes you feel most loved?
2. When did you first realize you had feelings for me?
3. What is your favorite memory of us together?
4. How do you most like to receive affection?
5. What song reminds you of us?
6. What is something I do that always makes you smile?
7. Where would you love to travel with me someday?
8. What is one thing you hope never changes about us?
9. What does your ideal quiet evening together look like?
10. What is something you admire about how I handle challenges?

---

## Fun

11. If we could only eat one cuisine for the rest of our lives, what would you choose?
12. What is the most embarrassing thing that happened on one of our dates?
13. If we had a couples reality show, what would it be called?
14. What movie could you watch with me over and over again?
15. If we won the lottery tomorrow, what is the first thing we would do together?
16. What is my most predictable habit?
17. If we were a famous duo, who would we be?
18. What is something silly that you secretly love about me?
19. If we opened a business together, what would it be?
20. What is one hobby you would love for us to try together?

---

## Deep

21. What is something you have never told anyone else but feel safe telling me?
22. What is your biggest fear about the future?
23. How do you want to be remembered by the people you love?
24. What is a belief you held strongly in the past but have since changed your mind about?
25. What does feeling truly understood look like to you?
26. What part of your childhood shaped who you are today?
27. What is one thing you wish more people understood about you?
28. When do you feel most at peace?
29. What is something you are still learning about yourself?
30. If you could go back and give your younger self one piece of advice, what would it be?

---

## Long-Distance

31. What do you miss most about being physically together?
32. What is something small I could do to make the distance feel shorter?
33. What time of day do you think about me the most?
34. What is the first thing you want to do when we see each other again?
35. How can I better support you from far away?
36. What is something you have learned about yourself during our time apart?
37. What is a new routine we could start to feel more connected?
38. What moment from our last visit together still makes you smile?
39. What is something you want us to experience together once the distance is over?
40. How has being long-distance changed the way you think about our relationship?

---
